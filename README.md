### 02. Finance Data Analytics  
SQL- and Python-style analytics focused on financial visibility, data integrity, variance analysis, marketing ROI, and reporting automation.

### 03. BI & Executive Dashboards

### 04. Accounting & Pricing Systems  
Operational finance frameworks covering AP and expense control, payroll reconciliation, and pricing and margin analysis.

### 05. Machine Learning Applied to Finance  
Practical ML use cases evaluated alongside traditional FP&A approaches, with emphasis on interpretability and business usability.

### 06. Investment & Valuation (UAE)
