# Financial Accounting Model Architecture

## Overview

This project demonstrates how a **financial accounting system** can be structured using Excel to simulate the workflow of a real-world **ERP accounting environment**.

The model follows a structured **accounting data pipeline** where operational data flows through multiple layers before producing standardized financial statements and performance analytics.

The objective of the project is to show how accounting data moves from **raw transactions to financial reporting and financial analysis**.

---

## Business Scenario

**NovaTech Commerce Ltd** is a simulated digital services company that generates revenue from product sales and business clients.

The company requires a structured accounting model to:

• record operational transactions  
• organize accounting records  
• generate financial statements  
• analyze financial performance  

This project models the accounting workflow required to support these financial processes.

---

## Accounting Model Architecture

The financial model is structured into four main layers that mirror real-world accounting system design.

---

### Data Layer

This layer captures the **raw operational data** used by the accounting system.

Tables included:

• Customers  
• Products  
• Sales_Transactions  
• Chart_of_Accounts  

These tables act as the **foundational data sources** for the entire accounting model.

---

### Transaction Processing Layer

Operational data is transformed into structured accounting records.

Sheets included:

• Invoice_Summary  
• General_Ledger  

At this stage:

• sales transactions are aggregated into invoices  
• transactions are mapped to accounting accounts  
• financial activity is recorded in the general ledger  

---

### Financial Statement Layer

This layer summarizes the accounting data into standard financial reports.

Sheets included:

• Trial_Balance  
• Profit_and_Loss  
• Retained_Earnings  
• Balance_Sheet  
• Cash_Flow  

These reports provide a full view of the company's **profitability, financial position, and cash movements**.

---

### Control & Analytics Layer

This layer focuses on financial validation and performance monitoring.

Sheets included:

• Bank_Reconciliation  
• Financial_KPIs  

This section helps with:

• verifying bank balances  
• validating financial records  
• monitoring financial performance indicators  

---

## Accounting Data Flow

The accounting system follows a structured financial workflow:

Sales Transactions  
↓  
Invoice Summary  
↓  
General Ledger  
↓  
Trial Balance  
↓  
Financial Statements  

• Profit & Loss  
• Balance Sheet  
• Cash Flow  
• Retained Earnings  

↓  
Financial KPIs
