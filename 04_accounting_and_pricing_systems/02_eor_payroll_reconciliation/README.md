# EOR Payroll Reconciliation

## Business Question
How can payroll invoices from external providers be reconciled accurately and efficiently?

## Decision Use
Supports finance and accounting teams by:
- validating payroll invoices
- ensuring correct tax and benefit treatment
- accelerating month-end close

## Scope
- Gross pay reconciliation
- Taxes and benefits validation
- Contract vs invoice comparison

## Controls & Validation
- Automated reconciliation logic
- Exception reporting for mismatches
- Standardized journal entry mapping

## Outputs
- Reconciliation summaries
- Exception reports
- Payroll journal entries

## Tools
Excel

## Status
Design documented.
