# Pricing & Margin Frameworks

## Business Question
How do pricing decisions affect margin, profitability, and financial sustainability?

## Decision Use
Used to support:
- pricing strategy evaluation
- margin protection
- profitability analysis by product or segment

## Methodology
- Contribution margin analysis
- Price sensitivity scenarios
- Cost structure linkage

## Controls & Validation
- Consistent cost definitions
- Separation of fixed and variable costs
- Sensitivity checks on pricing assumptions

## Outputs
- Pricing scenario comparisons
- Margin impact summaries
- Profitability diagnostics

## Tools
Excel

## Status
Framework documented.
