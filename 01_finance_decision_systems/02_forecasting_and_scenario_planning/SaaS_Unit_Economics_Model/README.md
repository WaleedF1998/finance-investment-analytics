# SaaS Unit Economics & Growth Viability (FP&A)

## Business Question
Is growth creating long-term value, or only increasing revenue without sustainable profitability?

## Decision Use
Supports decisions on:
- scaling customer acquisition
- pricing strategy
- growth vs efficiency trade-offs

## Model Structure
1. Customer roll-forward:
   - Opening customers
   - New customers
   - Churn
   - Ending customers

2. Revenue drivers:
   - ARPU assumptions
   - Paying customer mix

3. Unit economics:
   - Gross and contribution margin
   - CAC
   - Payback period
   - LTV / CAC

## Scenarios
- Base case
- Upside case
- Downside case

## Validation
- Consistent metric definitions
- Assumptions clearly documented
- Sensitivity checks on key drivers
