# Marketing ROI & Growth Analytics

## Business Question
Which marketing channels generate profitable growth, and which destroy value?

## Decision Use
Supports finance and growth teams in:
- reallocating marketing budgets
- evaluating CAC efficiency
- linking spend to revenue and margin impact

## Methodology
- Funnel analysis: Spend → CAC → Conversion → Revenue
- Channel-level unit economics
- Contribution margin comparison by channel

## Validation & Controls
- Consistent CAC definitions
- Separation of fixed and variable costs
- Sensitivity checks on conversion assumptions

## Outputs
- Channel ROI comparison
- Budget reallocation scenarios
- Margin impact analysis

## Tools
Python, SQL, Tableau / Power BI

## Status
Design documented. Partial analysis implemented.
