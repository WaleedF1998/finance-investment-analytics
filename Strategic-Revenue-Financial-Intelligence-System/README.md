# Strategic Revenue & Financial Intelligence System

## Overview

This project builds an integrated financial intelligence framework for a $35M ARR B2B SaaS company operating in a high-growth environment.

It connects revenue driver modeling, SQL-based reporting architecture, and capital structure simulation to demonstrate how operating performance translates into enterprise value and investor returns.

---

## System Architecture

### 1️⃣ Revenue Intelligence Engine
- MRR & ARR modeling  
- Churn & Net Revenue Retention (NRR)  
- Cohort retention analysis  
- LTV & CAC modeling  
- Monte Carlo growth simulation  

### 2️⃣ SQL Enterprise Data Layer
- Star schema design (`dim_customer`, `fact_subscription`)  
- KPI reporting queries  
- Revenue segmentation  
- Revenue-at-risk analysis  

### 3️⃣ Capital & LBO Engine
- ARR multiple valuation framework  
- Growth sensitivity modeling  
- Capital allocation comparison  
- 5-year LBO simulation  
- IRR & MOIC analysis  

---

## Strategic Insight

Retention efficiency and expansion strength are the primary drivers of sustainable growth and long-term enterprise value creation.
