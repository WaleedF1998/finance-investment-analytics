# Churn & LTV Modeling

## Business Question
Which customer behaviors drive churn and lifetime value?

## Decision Use
Supports decisions on:
- customer retention strategy
- pricing and packaging
- acquisition efficiency

## Analytical Approach
- Cohort-based churn analysis
- Feature selection based on customer behavior
- LTV estimation linked to retention patterns

## Validation & Controls
- Out-of-sample testing
- Comparison with historical averages
- Business sense-checks on outputs

## Outputs
- Churn risk indicators
- LTV estimates by segment
- Retention improvement scenarios

## Tools
Python

Framework documented.
