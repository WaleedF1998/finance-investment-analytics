# Board & Executive Reporting

## Business Question
What information does senior leadership need to make strategic decisions?

## Decision Use
Designed for board meetings and executive updates to:
- review financial performance
- assess risks and assumptions
- evaluate scenario outcomes

## Key Focus Areas
- Revenue and profitability trends
- Forecast vs plan
- Key risks and sensitivities
- Strategic initiatives impact

## Design Principles
- Simplicity and clarity
- Consistent definitions
- Narrative aligned with financials

## Tools
Tableau, Power BI

## Status
Reporting framework documented. Visual design planned.
