# Revenue & Margin Dashboards

## Business Question
How is revenue performing, and what is driving margin changes over time?

## Decision Use
Designed for executives and finance leaders to:
- monitor revenue growth
- understand margin trends
- identify price vs volume effects

## Key Metrics
- Total revenue
- Revenue growth rate
- Gross margin
- Contribution margin
- Price vs volume impact

## Dashboard Design
- High-level KPI summary
- Trend analysis over time
- Driver breakdown where needed

## Tools
Tableau, Power BI

## Status
Dashboard logic and layout documented. Visual implementation planned.
